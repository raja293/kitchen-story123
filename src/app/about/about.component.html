WHO WE ARE


At Go kart, it is our core values that guide all aspects of our business and the way we conduct ourselves. We strive to do the right thing, in the right way at all times. Here’s what being a Flipster is all about.


In 2007, we started the Go kart journey by selling books online. But now, over a decade later, our story is quite different. Today, Go Kart is India’s largest online marketplace and one of India’s leading technology powerhouses that is disrupting the way India shops online. What has got us here? The dynamic, passionate and talented folks we call Flipsters!
